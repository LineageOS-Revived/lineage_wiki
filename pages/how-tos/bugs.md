## What not to report
  - Bugs in unofficial builds or anything not downloaded from [our official portal](https://download.lineage.rgbroms.top/) - this includes other ROMs "based on" LineageOS Revived as well!
  - Missing builds
  - Asking for installation help
  - Asking for device support
  - Feature requests

## Reporting a bug

Before you submit a bug, please search to verify that someone else hasn't reported it already.  
Since this is just a small hobby project do not expect any timely fixes.  
We are reachable on [Telegram](https://t.me/rgb_chat).

{%- capture content %}[Logcats]({{ "how-to/logcat/" | relative_url }}) *must* be attached for all Android bugs, and *must* be captured right after reproducing the issue.{% endcapture %}
